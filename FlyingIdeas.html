Flying Ideas

My Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop,
Adobe Illustrator
Duration: 1 months

Introduction

Flying Ideas is an interior design and renovation company dedicated to delivering beautiful spaces and functional solutions. They offer online briefings and advice on renovation services, as well as offering to sell their own products.

Objective

Improve the user experience of online shopping. Help users save the time and cost of finding and buying things, and can easily navigate. Users can easily view the logistics status and purchase records. The design of the website is clear, rich in contents, modular and flexible.

Competitive Analysis

I conducted competitive analysis on thedesigners.space and moshinterior with focus on UX analysis: to evaluate their navigation, layout and general usability. This helped me understand what Flying Ideas needs to provide to stand out from the competition.

Recommended three main services that Flyideas can offer and users care about the most.
Recommended completed projects for users’ reference .
User’s reviews.

Persona

Rachel is an accountant , Married with 1 kids. Her house has just been decorated and needs some furniture. She doesn’t have time to shop in physical stores, so she buys furniture and accessories online.

Motivations

Her house has just been decorated and needs some furniture.

Pain point

She doesn’t have time to shop in physical stores.

User Flow

Low & Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

After creating the user flow diagram I began drawing some inital screen designs with pen and paper. This exercise allowed me to get down a lot of initial ideas and quickly create variations of the same screens. When I finished sketching I produced the first digital version of the design using Miro, the wireframing web app.

User Testing

Using digital medium-fidelity wireframes, I created an initial working prototype with Abobe XD for participants to use during user testing. Through the section “What we Do”, users can clearly know what kind of services the company’s website can provide, such as buying furniture and accessories, and providing interior design services. Users can also learn about the company’s success project, reviews and so on. When it comes to online shopping, user can quickly find what kind of products they want through product categories side navigation. For users with clear goals, search engines can also quickly find the products they want.

Branding Definition

The brand’s voice is friendly, reliable and informative. The brand want to bring memorable and enriching experience for home owners.

The design is based on competition and creativity, according to the research process. After this process was completed, a mood board was created to convey Flyingideas unique identity and voice.

On this solid foundation, graphic designs, ICONS and color palettes are determined. The result is a friendly, reliable， modern and artistic.

Primary Color

The whole site uses light yellow to do the keynote and Less area of black, better display picture content. Give a person fresh, modern and elegant feeling. Using a primary red color to match the product on the site is consistent and critical to brand identity and consistency.

Secondary Color

Secondary colors need to stand out from primary colors and should be used sparingly in certain areas that stand out and stand out.

Image style

The image associated with art and modern decoration style should be credible, artistic and modern.

High-Fidelity Wireframes

After completing a round of user testing I began working on the high-fidelity wireframes using Adobe XD, making sure to include important features that had been requested by the participants. It was at this stage that I introduced the brand colors and imagery to the design so it would evolve into the finished product.
