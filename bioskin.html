Bioskin

A responsive website provide users with a beautiful shopping experience.

Bioskin is now a one stop beauty centre providing an extensive range of premium treatments and solutions. They offer online briefings and advice on beauty services, as well as offering to sell their own products.

My Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
Duration: 1 months

Objective

Improve the user experience of online shopping. Help users save the time and cost of finding and buying things, and can easily navigate. Users can easily view the logistics status and purchase records. The design of the website is clear, rich in contents, modular and flexible.

Competitive Analysis

I conducted competitive analysis on mariannebeauty and newyorkskinsolutions with focus on UX analysis: to evaluate their navigation, layout and general usability. This helped me understand what Bioskin needs to provide to stand out from the competition.

Recommended services that new users care about the most.
Recommended skin care products.
Promotional products and services that users care about.

Persona

Tiffany is a IT company staff, Married with 2 kids. She is a manager who has a very tight schedule day. She often works overtime, and her skin condition is not very good, so she needs to do skin care and buy skin care products frequently.

Motivations

Due to working late, her skin condition is not very good, she needs to do skin care and buy some skin care products to keep her skin healthy.

Pain point

As a new user, she does not know what skincare services she needs and needs to know the functions and prices of skincare products.

User Flow

Low & Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

After creating the user flow diagram I began drawing some inital screen designs with pen and paper. This exercise allowed me to get down a lot of initial ideas and quickly create variations of the same screens. When I finished sketching I produced the first digital version of the design using Miro, the wireframing web app.

User Testing

Using digital medium-fidelity wireframes, I created an initial working prototype with Abobe XD for participants to use during user testing. When it comes to online shopping and consulting services, many people are interested in signature services and star products, so that they can quickly find the hottest products and services provided by the company, they want to experience the best projects and hot products. For users with clear goals, search engines can also quickly find the products or services they want.

Branding Definition

The brand’s voice is friendly, reliableand informative. The brand is widely known by women and is An established and trusted brand name.

The design is based on competition and creativity, according to the research process. After this process was completed, a mood board was created to convey Bioskin’s unique identity and voice.

On this solid foundation, graphic designs, ICONS and color palettes are determined. The result is a friendly, reliable, mature brand feel, but still approachable.

Primary Color

Bioskin’s brand color and primary product packaging are red, so using a primary red color to match the product on the site is consistent and critical to brand identity and consistency.

Secondary Color

Secondary colors need to stand out from primary colors and should be used sparingly in certain areas that stand out and stand out. An example of this is product promotional labels.

Image style

The image associated with the Bioskin brand should be trusted, mature, modern, product - and service-centric, and project a stylish feel.

The Bioskin brand helps women better protect their skin and body in the modern world, so it uses images of healthy women and skin care products to give users an example.

High-Fidelity Wireframes

After completing a round of user testing I began working on the high-fidelity wireframes using Adobe XD, making sure to include important features that had been requested by the participants. It was at this stage that I introduced the brand colors and imagery to the design so it would evolve into the finished product.
