Design and build of an e-commerce web experience for SAKE company

Introduction

Mizore offers a wide variety of sake from different prefectures in Japan, including a range of year-round and seasonal sake. Committed to creating a culture of belonging that welcomes everyone and positively impacts the customers they serve. They asked us to create a digitally responsive e-commerce site for them so that more people could get to know them and buy their products.

My Role: UI/UX Designer
Tools: Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
Duration: 1 months

Creative direction

They like simple, modern style. I conducted competitive on their similar industry website and was influenced by their simple design direction. Lots of white space and light grey background are the main colors to make the product picture stand out. Minimalist grids, lots of white space, product images and simple typographic elements are mixed together, with a modern flair brought out by thin line fonts.

Competitive Analysis

I conducted competitive analysis on mariannebeauty and newyorkskinsolutions with focus on UX analysis: to evaluate their navigation, layout and general usability. This helped me understand what Bioskin needs to provide to stand out from the competition.

Recommended services that new users care about the most.

Promotional products and services that users care about.
